About

Turning research ideas into working systems.

I'm a Computer Science & Engineering undergraduate at Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET), working at the intersection of deep learning, computer vision, and natural language processing.

My first-author IEEE paper introduced an attention-enhanced pipeline — EfficientNetB3 with CBAM and multi-scale feature fusion — for high-accuracy image classification. I'm currently researching language-invariant emotion detection across Bangla, Banglish, and English using contrastive learning.

I care about the full arc of research: problem formulation, model design, experimentation, and careful evaluation. I'm especially drawn to large language models, multimodal AI, and intelligent systems for low-resource languages.
